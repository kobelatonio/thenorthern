FURNITURE

By combining the Scandinavian’s ideals of craftsmanship with a modern design language, THE NORTHERN has a simple yet refined expression.

Our furniture has a visually resonant design that performs its intended function easily.

OUTLINE SERIES

by Anderssen & Voll

The Outline Series adds new perspectives to the classic Scandinavian design sofas of the 1960's, marrying the ideals of simplicity and function into one.

With the design taking its name from its strong outline of a sofa, the Outline Series is clean and elegant on the outside with a deep seat and soft cushioning on its inside.

LIGHTING

Our lighting offers the opportunity to create the perfect balance of function and form.

The diverse range of pendant, desk and floor lamps bring you lighting solutions that are both utilitarian and an integral part of Nordic interior design.

PENDANT LAMP

by Benjamin Hubert

The Pendant Lamp is designed with soft, playful forms for a modern expression. The design provides both diffused lighting and direct lighting, the latter through its top and bottom opening.

ACCESSORIES

With a modern and friendly way to add storage to any home or professional space, our accessories have a sculptural expression, making them both an object of decoration and use.

Everything brings a contemporary form through a combination of traditional craftsmanship and playful design language.

view accessories

TWINE CUSHION

by Aiayu

With a refined and modern expression, the Twine Cushion has an invitingly tactile character with distinct knitted pebbles, joined by the softness of its baby llama wool material.

The wool of its outer has been sustainably sourced in Bolivia with the factory cleaning 100% of its water post-production while reusing 75% of it.
